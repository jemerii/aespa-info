INFORMATION

        aespa (에스파) is a 4-member South Korean girl group under SM Entertainment, consisting of Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning. 
        They made their Korean debut on November 17, 2020, with the digital single, “Black Mamba“. They made their Japanese debut on July 3, 2024 with the single, “Hot Mess“. 
        On June 2, 2022, it was announced that aespa has signed with Warner Records for global promotions.
